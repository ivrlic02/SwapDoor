## **Assignment Overview**
This assignment documents the design evolution of SwapDoor, a global home exchange platform, through the development of structured low and high-fidelity prototypes.
The workflow integrates detailed user personas and information architecture to ensure the interface is intuitive and accessible for travelers, families, and digital nomads alike.
By applying modern minimalist aesthetics and CRAP design principles, the prototype delivers a high-impact visual experience that prioritizes community trust and seamless navigation.
